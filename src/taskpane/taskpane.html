Use these custom functions directly in your spreadsheet cells.

BOC.RATE
=BOC.RATE(currency, [date])
Daily rate — latest or on a specific date.
=BOC.RATE("USD")
=BOC.RATE("EUR", "2024-03-31")
BOC.MONTHLY_AVG
=BOC.MONTHLY_AVG(currency, year, month)
Monthly average rate published by BOC.
=BOC.MONTHLY_AVG("USD", 2024, 3)
BOC.ANNUAL_AVG
=BOC.ANNUAL_AVG(currency, year)
Annual average rate for a full year.
=BOC.ANNUAL_AVG("USD", 2023)
BOC.TO_CAD
=BOC.TO_CAD(amount, currency, [date])
Convert foreign currency amount to CAD.
=BOC.TO_CAD(5000, "USD", "2024-12-31")
BOC.FROM_CAD
=BOC.FROM_CAD(amount, currency, [date])
Convert CAD amount to a foreign currency.
=BOC.FROM_CAD(10000, "EUR")
BOC.RATE_DATE
=BOC.RATE_DATE(currency, [date])
Returns the actual date of the rate used (helpful for weekend/holiday dates).
=BOC.RATE_DATE("USD", "2024-12-28")
